Paris, France

I was able to visit my cousins in France last summer! It was my first time ever visiting. Although my trip was short (only 3 days!) they made sure I was able to see all of the main attractions. I was able to see the Louvre, the Place des Vosges, the Eiffel Tower, and much more! What shone in France was definitely the pastries. The pan de chocolat, eclairs, and macarons in France were amazing, especially for someone who has a sweet tooth like I do! While I felt like I saw a lot in the small period of time I was there, I'd love to be able to go back again and see what I've missed. It was great having my cousins as locals to help me navigate and be my built in tour guides! - Rina
