Prishtina, Kosovo


I tend to visit Kosovo pretty often since my parents are refugees from here. The rest of my family lives in the region, so it's always nice going back and seeing them. I always feel the most at home when I'm back in Prishtina because the food, the people, and the language are so familiar to me. The cuisine is great, and the prices are unmatched. The nature and scenery is also beautiful, since there's everything from the city, the countryside, and the mountains in Kosovo. I typically enjoy a homecooked meal but I'd occasionally go out for dinner once in a while. I can't wait for the next time I'm able to return! - Rina
