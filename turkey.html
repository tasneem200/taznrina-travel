Istanbul, Turkey

I visited Istanbul, Turkey during this past December of 2023. My cousins live there and know the language, which made it really easy to navigate overall, since they were like my personal tour guides. Everything there was breathtakingly beautiful and I loved it so much. Some of my favorite places include Cups and Clouds Coffee (the Sahleb there is amazing), EspressoLab. As for Turkish cuisine, I tried lahmacun for the first time, which I really liked, as well as Pide and Cig Kofte (which kinda made my stomach hurt lol). Overall, this was such a beautiful trip and I would love to return soon, as I feel like there's still so much I haven't done there! - Taz
