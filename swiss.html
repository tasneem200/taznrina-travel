Zurich, Switzerland

I stopped by in Switzerland in the same trip that I went to France! I also have family in Zurich, so I ended up staying with my uncle and cousins. I stayed here a bit longer than France and my trip was about a week and a half. I loved how walkable Switzerland was. There were so many scenic areas that felt straight out of a movie, and there was very little need to drive in general. It felt very peaceful and calm, even in the middle of the city. The public transit, while I never took it, seemed very clean and up-to-date. My family also had a shed in the mountains where they tended to their own garden, which was a very pretty sight to see. I'm hoping to stop by again when I'm abroad in the fall! - Rina
