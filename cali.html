San Francisco, California

I visited San Francisco during the Spring of 2023, for a quick 3-day trip for a pre-professional program I was doing. It was honestly amazing. We went on a day trip during our last day there, where we tried as many of the tourist attractions as possible. We got to see the Golden Gate Bridge at night, which was so pretty, the painted ladies (the houses from Full House), the seals at Pier 39, the Palace of Arts, and we had an amazing sushi dinner. 10/10 would recommend visiting, it was so so fun! - Taz
